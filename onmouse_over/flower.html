Flowers

Rose

The rose is a popular flower known for its vibrant colors and sweet fragrance. It comes in shades like red, pink, white, and yellow. Roses are often considered a symbol of love and romance.

Scientific Name: Rosa

Lotus

The lotus is a sacred flower often associated with purity and spiritual enlightenment. It thrives in aquatic environments, blooming in colors like pink and white.

Scientific Name: Nelumbo nucifera

Tulip

The tulip is a graceful flower often associated with love and renewal. It thrives in temperate climates, blooming in a variety of colors like red, yellow, and purple.

Scientific Name: Tulipa
